OverView

This two-year program is designed to educate students on electronics within the aviation industry. Students will learn avionics installation, troubleshooting, system integration and avionics operating systems, including GPS systems, autopilot, communication systems, and weather radar.

West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.

SkillsUSA

"Preparing for leadership in the world of work"

SkillsUSA is a United States career and technical student organization. Formed in 1965, with an initial name of Vocatioanl Industrial Clubs of America (VICA).

The 3 career essentials include:

  1. 1. Foundation: including lesson plans based on Common Core State Standards.
  2. 2. Experience: providing real-world context for the important elements of the SkillsUSA Framework of developing personal, workplace, and technical skills essential in academics.
  3. 3. Assesments: reliable evaluation of numerous technical and employability areas.
